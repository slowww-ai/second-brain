# Atomic Notes

An **atomic note** captures exactly one idea — small enough to state in a
sentence, complete enough to stand on its own. It's the unit the
[[zettelkasten]] is built from, and the granularity a [[second-brain]] needs to
stay reusable.

## Why one idea per note

- **Linkable** — a note about a single idea can be referenced precisely from
  many places. A note about five ideas can't.
- **Recombinable** — atomic notes are the "intermediate packets" you reassemble
  into briefs and posts without starting from a blank page.
- **Findable** — search returns a clean hit instead of a wall of mixed topics.

> If a note covers two ideas, split it. (This template's `/garden` command flags
> notes that have grown too broad.)
