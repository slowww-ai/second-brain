# Second Brain

A **second brain** is an external, trusted store for the ideas and references
you'd otherwise try — and fail — to keep in your head. The mind is for having
ideas, not holding them; the system holds them so you can think with them later.

## CODE

Tiago Forte frames the workflow as **CODE**:

- **Capture** what resonates (not everything).
- **Organize** for action — by the project a note serves, not by category.
- **Distill** to the essence so a busier future you can use it fast. This is
  where [[atomic-notes]] and [[evergreen-notes]] come in.
- **Express** — turn notes into output. A vault you never use is just hoarding.

## Why it compounds

The payoff isn't storage, it's **retrieval and reuse**. Like the
[[zettelkasten]], value emerges when notes are linked: opening one pulls the
related thoughts along with it. Knowledge that produces output is the only
knowledge that compounds.
